Palo Alto Library, Monthly Book Sale

Monthly Used Books Sales

More than 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines and more in at our monthly sale!

Hardbound/Paperback Books
Hardbound books- $1 each
Paperback books - 50 cents each

Hardbound/Paperback Books are in Main Room. It is along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center.

Children's book, music,videos and toys
Hardbound books- $1
Many Items- 50 cents or $1

Children Books are in New Room. It is near the Cubberley track, Pavilion and the Greedell School.

Bargain Books
Hardbound Books - $1
Paperback Books - 50 cents
Children Books - 25 cents

Bargai Books are in Room K6 and K7.

On Sunday, prices are half off and Grocery bags for $5 each . Also, purchase gift certificates at the sale in $5 deominations.
Come early on Saturday to ensure an early place in line. We allow only 160 customers into the Main room at a time.

On Saturday, Children's and Bargain books are open an hour earlier than Main Room. Also, during first hour of sale, please select no more than 12 books at a time and bring to the cashiers to hold until you are ready to pay for all your purchases.

Please bring your large books for donation. Our 100% of profit goes to help local libraries with no sales tax on your purchases.
